Item Arrows mod. Forge Jam 2022 submission.

Craft an envelope, then craft items with the envelope to put them in the envelope.

Craft a precision blunt arrow [does no damage to hit entities, flies the same every time], then attach the envelope to the arrow.

The arrow will insert into any hit item handler blocks, and certain entities [chest minecarts etc work - try attaching wheat and shooting a cow]

An arrow collector block is provided to gather any missed arrows.

An advanced dispenser is provided to aim in any direction and shoot further.

Blocks are provided to automate putting items in envelopes and attaching envelopes to arrows.
